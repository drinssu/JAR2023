Call for Papers: The Journal of Academic Research (JAR) is a peer-reviewed journal published by the Samar State University (SSU). JAR will cater to researches written by educators and students alike, especially those but not limited to the higher education institutions (HEIs) in the Philippines and other parts of the world. JAR articles are subjected to peer-review by at least two content reviewers.
